Working with Folium

Using Folium we (me and Pedro) made some studies about accidents in Brazil federal highways based on the open data of PRF. We've used the data from January 2017 through May 2019.

Here are some results:

  • Choropleth map of accidents by state.
  • Accidents cluster map of the states with more and less occurrences.
  • General heatmap of accidents in Brazil.
  • Bar chart of accidents by highway.
  • Heatmap of accidents of the 10 highways with more occurrences.
  • Heatmap with time of accidents in Brazil by month.
  • Map generated in the Kepler indicating the fatal victims.
